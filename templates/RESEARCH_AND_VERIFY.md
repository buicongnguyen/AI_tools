# Research and Verification Protocol

Question: **[QUESTION]**

Verification date: **[DATE]**

## Method

- Define terms and geographic/time scope.
- Search multiple query formulations.
- Prefer official primary sources.
- Open and read every cited source.
- Cross-check material claims with an independent source when possible.
- Record source date and distinguish fact from inference.
- Explicitly report conflicts and missing evidence.
